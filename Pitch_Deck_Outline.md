# QBridge - Pitch Deck Outline

### Slide 1: The Real-World Problem
**The Dilemma:** Quantum hardware requires complex routing mapping and SWAP insertion to execute logical circuits.
**The Threat:** Designing quantum circuits is incredibly capital intensive. Corporate espionage threatens proprietary quantum circuit designs during their transmission across classical internet networks. Protecting unoptimized, physical topologies before they ever hit physical execution hardware is mission critical.

### Slide 2: The QBridge Solution
**The Vision:** We introduce a Secure Compiled API Tunnel.
We aren't just routing qubits. QBridge creates a state-of-the-art enterprise transmission buffer. We protect your proprietary data in transit while executing high-performance compilations specifically optimized matching your hardware availability matrix.

### Slide 3: How It Works
**The Flow:**
1. **ANU Entropy:** The system pulls true quantum vacuum fluctuations from ANU.
2. **AES-GCM WebSocket/REST API:** That entropy generates cryptographically superior transmission protocols encrypting our endpoints.
3. **SWAP Routing Baseline:** Ingested payloads are rapidly processed through our compiler engine dictating fast, logical heuristic placement and SWAP evaluations.

### Slide 5: The Roadmap
**Future Innovations:**
Currently, our compiler excels using the Baseline Heuristic approach. However, because our entire framework embraces a modular API architecture, it represents a dynamic foundation.
